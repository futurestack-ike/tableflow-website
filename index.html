Features

Everything in one place

From the moment a customer sits down to the moment they pay — TableFlow handles it all.

📱
Any device, instantly

No app to download. Open the link on any phone, tablet or laptop. Your staff can start taking orders in minutes.

🍳
Live kitchen screen

Orders go to the kitchen instantly. Staff mark them Not Started → Busy → Ready. No more shouting across the kitchen.

🔔
Instant notifications

When the kitchen marks food as ready, a notification pops up on the floor staff's device. Table service becomes effortless.

💳
Fast cash & card payment

One tap for cash or card. The full order summary appears before confirming. All payments logged automatically.

📊
Owner dashboard

Revenue by day, week, month or year. Top selling dishes, dine-in vs takeaway, cash vs card — all in one PIN-protected screen.

🎨
Branded to your restaurant

TableFlow is configured with your restaurant's logo and colors. Your staff sees your brand every time they open the system — not a generic app.

How it works

Order to payment in 4 steps

1
🪑
Staff takes the order

Select table number or takeaway name. Tap items from the digital menu. Add notes. Done in under 30 seconds.

2
🍳
Kitchen gets notified

The order appears instantly on the kitchen screen. The chef moves it from New → Busy → Ready as they cook.

3
🔔
Staff is alerted

A push notification fires on the server's device the moment the food is ready. No walkie-talkies, no shouting.

4
💰
Payment collected

Tap cash or card. Confirm. Done. Revenue is recorded automatically. Owner sees it in the report dashboard.
